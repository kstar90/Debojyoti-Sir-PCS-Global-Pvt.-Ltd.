DLF stands for Delhi Land and Finance

Delhi Land and Finance is one of the largest commercial real estate developers in India. It is also engaged in the business of generation of power provision of maintenance services hospitality and recreational activities life insurance and retail chain outlets. Its internal business includes the development business and rental business. The development business of the Company is involved in the sale of residential spaces select commercial offices and commercial complexes. The company has a unique business model with earnings arising from development and rentals.
